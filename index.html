Tirumala and Tirupati are famous pilgrimage places in Andhra Pradesh, India. Tirumala is located on the green Seshachalam Hills and is surrounded by forests, fresh air, and beautiful nature. The calm environment gives peace to both the mind and soul. The main attraction of Tirumala is the Sri Venkateswara Temple, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara. Every day, thousands of devotees visit the temple to pray and seek blessings. People believe that the Lord fulfills true wishes, which makes this temple very special.
